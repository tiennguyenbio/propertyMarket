## What I learned
- Postcodes are not static, they change over time. ABS defines 'postcode' is only for mail delivery. 
- The lastest postcode mapping by ABS was produced in 2011, which is outdated compared to the postcode we are using in 2025: https://www.abs.gov.au/AUSSTATS/abs@.nsf/DetailsPage/1270.0.55.006July%202011?OpenDocument
- Australia Statistical Area Level (SA1-SA4) are geographic units used for census data, classified by size of population. In which, SA2 names represent suburbs or group of suburbs name.
